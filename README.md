# 🛒 Blinkit Sales Analysis – Business Intelligence Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing Blinkit’s grocery sales data to deliver actionable business insights through an interactive Power BI dashboard. The objective is to enable stakeholders to monitor sales performance, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Blinkit operates across multiple outlets, product categories, and locations. However, there is no centralized reporting system to effectively track:

* Sales performance across outlet types and sizes
* Product category contribution to revenue
* Customer preferences and buying behavior
* Key trends impacting business growth

This project addresses these challenges by building a comprehensive business reporting dashboard.

---

## 🧠 Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance and key revenue drivers
* Identify top and underperforming product categories
* Evaluate outlet performance based on size and location
* Provide dynamic and interactive reporting for stakeholders

---

## 📊 Key KPIs

* **Total Sales:** $1.20M
* **Average Sales per Item**
* **Average Customer Rating**
* **Total Number of Items Sold:** 8,523

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI** – Dashboard development & visualization
* **Excel (CSV)** – Data source
* **DAX** – KPI calculations
* **Power Query** – Data cleaning and transformation

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

* Interactive filters for outlet size, location, and product category
* KPI cards for quick performance overview
* Category-wise and outlet-wise sales analysis
* Trend analysis across years
* Dynamic metric selection using slicers

---

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

### 📌 Sales Performance

* Total revenue generated is **$1.20M**
* **Fruits & Vegetables** is the top-performing category ($178K)
* **Seafood** is the lowest-performing category ($9K)

### 📌 Outlet Analysis

* Medium-sized outlets generate the highest revenue (**$507K**)
* High-sized outlets underperform compared to expectations

### 📌 Product Trends

* **Low-fat products** generate significantly higher sales than regular products
* Certain categories consistently outperform others, indicating strong customer preference

### 📌 Customer Behavior

* Average ratings across categories range between **3.8 – 4.0**
* Higher-rated categories show better sales consistency

### 📌 Time-Based Trends

* Sales peaked in **2018**, indicating a high-growth period

---

## 💡 Business Impact

* Enables targeted strategies for underperforming categories
